CAMPUS RENT MANAGEMENT SYSTEM
A secure College-Only Rental & Buy Platform

CampusRent is a secure, closed-campus digital marketplace designed exclusively for college students. 
The platform enables students to rent or buy academic and essential items within their own college ecosystem in a safe, verified, and affordable manner.
Unlike public marketplaces, CampusRent ensures trust, transparency, and verified access by allowing only college-domain email users to register and interact.
