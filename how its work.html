Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers

How do I sign up for CampusRent?

+

Simply click the "Sign Up" button, provide your college email address, and verify your student status. Once verified, you can start browsing and renting items immediately.

Is there a security deposit?

+

Some items may require a refundable security deposit, which will be specified in the item listing. The deposit is fully refunded upon successful return of the item in good condition.

What if the item gets damaged?

+

Normal wear and tear is expected. For significant damage, you may be responsible for repair costs, which would be deducted from your security deposit. We recommend treating rented items with care.

Can I extend my rental period?

+

Yes! If the item is available for the extended period, you can request an extension through your dashboard. The lender must approve the extension before it becomes official.

How do payments work?

+

All payments are processed securely through our platform. We hold the payment until the item is successfully picked up, ensuring protection for both renters and lenders.
