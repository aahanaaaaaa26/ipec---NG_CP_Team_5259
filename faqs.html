Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about CampusRent

Getting Started

How do I sign up for CampusRent?

+

Signing up is easy! Click the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner, enter your college email address, and verify your student status. You'll receive a verification email to confirm your account. Once verified, you can immediately start browsing and renting items from fellow students on your campus.

Who can use CampusRent?

+

CampusRent is exclusively for college and university students. You need a valid college email address (.edu or official college domain) to create an account. This ensures a safe, trusted community of verified students.

Is CampusRent free to use?

+

Yes! Creating an account and browsing items is completely free. We only charge a small service fee (5-10%) on successful rental transactions to maintain the platform and ensure quality service.

Which colleges are supported?

+

We currently support over 50 major colleges and universities across India, including all IITs, NITs, BITS, and many other institutions. If your college isn't listed, contact us and we'll work on adding it!

Renting Items

How do I rent an item?

+

Browse or search for the item you need, click on it to view details, select your rental duration, and click "Book Now". You'll be prompted to make payment through our secure gateway. Once confirmed, you can coordinate with the lender for pickup directly through our in-app messaging system.

What rental durations are available?

+

Rental durations are flexible and set by individual lenders. Common options include daily, weekly, monthly, or semester-long rentals. You can also request custom durations by messaging the lender directly.

Can I extend my rental period?

+

Yes! If you need the item for longer, you can request an extension from your dashboard. The lender must approve the extension, and additional payment will be calculated based on the extended duration. It's best to request extensions before your rental period ends.

What if the item is damaged or doesn't work?

+

Inspect the item during pickup and report any existing damage immediately through the app. If you discover issues after pickup, contact the lender and our support team within 24 hours. We'll help mediate and find a fair solution, which may include partial refunds or replacements.

How do I return an item?

+

Coordinate a return time and location with the lender through the messaging feature. Meet on campus, return the item in the same condition you received it, and both parties confirm the return in the app. You'll then be able to rate your experience.

Lending Items

How do I list an item for rent?

+

Click "List New Item" from your dashboard, upload clear photos, add a detailed description, set your rental price and duration options, and specify availability. Our team reviews all listings to ensure quality and appropriateness before they go live.

What items can I rent out?

+

You can rent textbooks, electronics (laptops, tablets, calculators), lab equipment, project kits (Arduino, Raspberry Pi), sports equipment, musical instruments, cameras, and other college-related items. Items must be legal, safe, and in good working condition. Prohibited items include weapons, illegal substances, and counterfeit goods.

How much can I earn by lending?

+

Earnings depend on your item, pricing, and rental frequency. Many students earn â‚¹2,000-10,000 per semester by renting out textbooks, electronics, and lab equipment. You set your own prices based on item value and market demand. After our service fee, you receive 90-95% of the rental amount.

What if a renter damages my item?

+

We recommend collecting a security deposit (refundable) for valuable items. If damage occurs beyond normal wear and tear, you can file a claim through our platform. We'll review the case and facilitate compensation from the security deposit or help you seek resolution. For high-value items, consider our optional damage protection insurance.

Can I remove or edit my listing?

+

Yes, you can edit listing details, pricing, or availability anytime from your dashboard. You can also mark items as unavailable or delete listings. However, you cannot remove active rentals - you must complete those commitments first.

Payments & Pricing

How do payments work?

+

All payments are processed securely through our platform using trusted payment gateways (Razorpay, PayTM, UPI). We hold the payment in escrow until you confirm successful pickup. Once confirmed, we release payment to the lender (minus our service fee). This protects both renters and lenders.

What payment methods are accepted?

+

We accept credit cards, debit cards, UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, PayTM), net banking, and digital wallets. Choose your preferred method during checkout. All transactions are encrypted and secure.

Is there a security deposit?

+

Security deposits are optional and set by individual lenders, typically for high-value items like laptops or cameras. The deposit is fully refundable upon successful return of the item in good condition. It's held separately and automatically refunded within 2-3 business days after return confirmation.

What is the service fee?

+

We charge a 5-10% service fee on the rental amount to maintain the platform, provide customer support, and ensure security. This fee is automatically included in the total amount shown at checkout. There are no hidden charges.

Can I get a refund?

+

Refund policies depend on the timing: Full refund if cancelled 24+ hours before pickup, 50% refund for 12-24 hours notice, no refund for cancellations within 12 hours of pickup. If the lender cancels or the item is significantly different from the listing, you'll receive a full refund.

Safety & Trust

How do you verify users?

+

All users must register with a valid college email address. We send a verification email to confirm the address belongs to a current student. Additionally, we verify college ID cards for enhanced trust. Users can build reputation through ratings and reviews from completed transactions.

Is it safe to meet strangers on campus?

+

Always meet in public, well-lit areas on campus (library, cafeteria, main quad). Bring a friend if you're uncomfortable. Never share personal information like your home address or room number. Trust your instincts - if something feels off, cancel the transaction and report it to our team.

What if I encounter a problem or scam?

+

Report any suspicious activity, scams, or problematic users immediately through the "Report" button on their profile or listings. Our team investigates all reports within 24 hours and takes appropriate action, including warnings, suspensions, or permanent bans. We take community safety seriously.

How does the rating system work?

+

After each transaction, both renters and lenders can rate each other on a 5-star scale and leave written reviews. Ratings appear on user profiles to help others make informed decisions. Consistently low-rated users may face account restrictions. Be honest but respectful in your reviews.

What data do you collect and how is it used?

+

We collect necessary information like name, email, college, and payment details to operate the platform. We never sell your data to third parties. All data is encrypted and stored securely. You can review our detailed Privacy Policy for complete information about data collection, usage, and your rights.
